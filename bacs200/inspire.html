Lebron James

Not only is Lebron James arguably the greatest basketball player of all time. He is also one of the greatest heroes this world has seen.

Lebron James has the following heroic characteristics.

  • Bravery - Confident behavior and character
  • Leadership - Leaders create change
  • Dedication - Always follows through with tasks and goals
  • Awareness - Ability to understand complex situations
  • Individuality - Distinguishable character

Lebron James holds many characteristics that classify him as a leader. He inspires the youth of society to strive for greatness and never give up. Lebron is not afraid to preach his beliefs on political issues and become a driving force for change. Lastly, Lebron's legacy will last generations and will continue to inspire more and more people.
